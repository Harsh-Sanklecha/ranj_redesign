Rahul Jain

Co-Founder

RAHUL JAIN is a founding partner of RANJ. Being a qualified Company Secretary from The ICSI, he administers matters pertaining to Corporate Compliance Management, Corporate Dispute Resolution, M&As, Contract law, Insolvency & Bankrupcy Legislation, Securities law.

Besides, he is a Trademark Agent and spearheading the IPR Division of the firm. In his quest to contribute to profession, he has served as the Vice-Chairman of ICSI-Hyderabad Chapter at a young age of 29. He is also a regular speaker for various Seminars, Competitions & Conferences at ICSI & top Law and Management Institutions of the Country.

Nitesh Agarwal

Co-Founder

NITESH AGARWAL is a founding partner of the firm. By qualification he is a Bachelor of Commerce and an Associate Member of The Institute of Company Secretaries of India (ICSI), a Statutory body under an act of Parliament.

He is a regular columnist on various updates on legal & corporate front and has featured in a various seminars and conferences.
